Hi, I am Martha Vue

Frontend Developer, based in the United States.

Hello, my name is Martha Vue. I was born and raised in the United States. My family comes from a small tribe in Laos called Hmong. With my family roots, technology was not a luxury available to my parents or grandparents. However, artistry was and is a talent that has been handed down from generation to generation.

I started off my career in accounting and am still working in the field 11 years after graduation. For the past few years though, I have taken an interest in learning how to code. Coding takes me back to 2nd grade as I was selected to work with Computer Software Engineers at IBM. I enjoyed it then and still enjoy it now. I look forward to implementing my inheritance of artistry to my work and making a career out of it.
